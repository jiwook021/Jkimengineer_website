About Jkimengineer.com website


Dedictated for EE&CS Engineering Guideline Reference by Professional Engineers


Hi, I am Jiwook Kim. I have spent the most time learning about the parts of reality I am most passionate about trying to understand: Applied Mathematics, Applied Physics, and Software Engineering. As an Engineer, I found the Fundamentals of Engineering important to build reliable, maintainable, and flexible products. So I made this website for myself and engineers to overview the fundamentals of EE/CS and apply the knowledge with proper guidance from professional engineers who created great lectures and books for engineers. The scopes of this website go from Mathematics to applied Mathematics, Physics to Hardware Electronics, and interaction between electronics and Computational Mathematics: Software.
